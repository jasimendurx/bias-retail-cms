About Us

A Legacy of Retail
Innovation & Prestige

BIAS Retail LLC is a dynamic retail franchise organization established in 2012 with a clear mission: to bring exceptional international brands to the Middle East and elevate the region's retail landscape.

With deep-rooted market expertise and a passion for innovation, we specialize in building and scaling select global brands across Fashion, Beauty, and Hospitality. Our portfolio includes American Vintage, The Face Shop UAE, and hospitality concepts associated with Alain Ducasse.
